# OnePlayer App Store copy

## what the app does
OnePlayer is a Safari extension for YouTube.
It does two things:

1. It gives you sticky Picture-in-Picture as you scroll.
2. It keeps only one YouTube tab playing at a time by pausing the others.

That’s the whole product.
It’s for people who end up with too many YouTube tabs open and want playback to stay under control.

## app store subtitle
Sticky Picture-in-Picture and one-tab-at-a-time playback for YouTube.

## app store description
OnePlayer is a Safari extension for YouTube.

It keeps video playback tidy in two ways:

- sticky Picture-in-Picture follows the video as you scroll
- only one YouTube tab plays at a time, so the other tabs pause automatically

If you keep too many YouTube tabs open, OnePlayer reduces the mess.
It does not try to be a general browser tool. It just handles YouTube playback better.
