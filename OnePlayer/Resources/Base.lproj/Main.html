OnePlayer

Turn on the Safari extension, allow access to YouTube, and manage playback from the toolbar.

1
Open Safari Settings Use the button below or press Command-comma in Safari.
2
Enable OnePlayer Open the Extensions pane and turn on OnePlayer for Safari.
3
Allow YouTube Access Choose “Always Allow on Every Website” for the smoothest playback control.
Auto-Pause and Picture in Picture settings are available from the Safari toolbar after activation.
