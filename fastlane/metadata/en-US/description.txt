OnePlayer is a Safari extension for YouTube that keeps video playback tidy.

- Sticky Picture-in-Picture — the video follows you as you scroll, so you can read comments and browse without losing sight of what's playing
- Auto-Pause — only one YouTube tab plays at a time, the others pause automatically
- PiP Button — a button on the video player to manually pop out any video to Picture-in-Picture

If you keep too many YouTube tabs open, OnePlayer reduces the mess. It doesn't try to be a general browser tool — it just handles YouTube playback better.

All features can be toggled on or off from the extension popup.
